NIKI

Nicole Zefanya, known professionally as NIKI, is an Indonesian singer, songwriter, and record producer under the label 88rising.

Nicole Zefanya, born 24 January 1999, grew up listening to the 1990s R&B such as Destiny's Child and Aaliyah because of her mother. As she was in school, Zefanya gained popularity with her covers and original songs on YouTube, gaining more than 40,000 subscribers. She has since deleted all her previous videos but some of her old songs can still be found online. In 2017, Zefanya moved to Nashville and studied Music in Lipscomb University while releasing “See U Never” and “I Like U” under the American mass media company 88rising as an artist under their label.

Through her music, Zefanya encourages Asians and Asian Americans to own their heritage. During the Head in The Clouds music festival, NIKI takes a moment to address the 10,000-plus people gathered in Los Angeles’ Historic State Park. “I just want to say, as an Asian female, I do not take this day and this stage for granted. My hope is that above everything else today, that you feel heard, you feel understood, but most of all that you feel represented." NIKI's mission is to empower young Asian artists under-represented in US music industry.
